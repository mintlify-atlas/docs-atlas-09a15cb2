## What are Agents?

Agents are pre-configured AI personalities and workflows designed for specific tasks. They can include:

- **Custom prompts** - Specialized instructions for particular development scenarios
- **Tool configurations** - Pre-configured tools and integrations
- **Workflow patterns** - Step-by-step processes for common tasks
- **Domain expertise** - Knowledge tailored to specific frameworks, languages, or platforms
